# **✨ The In‑Between Manifesto (Studio Version)**

**We build for the people who live between extremes — the hobbyists, the tinkerers, the curious minds who learn by doing.
 Not the pros chasing certifications.
 Not the enterprises drowning in process.
 Not the total beginners who want everything done for them.
 Just the people in the middle — the ones who think, explore, experiment, and create.**

**We believe tools should empower without overwhelming.
 Games should challenge without punishing.
 Software should teach without lecturing.
 And creativity should feel like discovery, not obligation.**

**We reject the bloat, the grind, the manipulation, the microtransactions, the “live service forever” treadmill.
 We reject the apps that assume you know nothing, and the tools that assume you know everything.**

**We build for the in‑between — the space where curiosity lives.
 Where learning feels natural.
 Where making things feels joyful.
 Where play feels meaningful.**

**Our mission is simple:
 Create tools and games that respect your intelligence, reward your curiosity, and give you room to explore.
 Not the extremes.
 Not the noise.
 Just the good stuff in between.**
